About Lamo Microfinance Bank

Transforming lives through accessible, innovative financial solutions

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and small businesses with accessible, innovative financial solutions that foster economic growth and financial inclusion across all communities we serve.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and accessible microfinance institution, recognized for our commitment to financial empowerment and sustainable development in every community we touch.

Our Journey

40+ years of building trust, serving communities, and revolutionizing finance

1980s

Foundation & Launch

Lamo Microfinance Bank was founded with a vision to provide accessible financial services to underserved communities. Starting with a small team, we began our mission to democratize banking.

1990s

Expansion & Growth

With growing success, we expanded to multiple branches across the nation. Our team grew from 50 to 500+ dedicated professionals committed to serving our customers.

2000s

Digital Innovation

We embraced digital transformation, launching our first online banking platform. This revolutionized how our customers access their accounts and manage their finances.

2010s-Present

Mobile-First Revolution

Today, we lead the industry with cutting-edge mobile banking technology, serving over 10 million users with 50+ branches nationwide, achieving numerous banking awards.
